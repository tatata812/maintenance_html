What is air leak inspection service, using a “sound-visible camera”?

Using special camera that detects and visualizes only the sound of air leaks, we find out air leaks throughout plant.
Previous methods to identify air leaks include spraying soapy water onto areas where air leaks are likely to occur and checking to see if air leak is occurring. However, there were issues such as it was difficult to detect only large air leaks that could be heard, and it was time consuming, therefore, only a part of plant could be inspected.
On the other hand, with this method of using special camera, air leaks can be detected simply by holding up the camera and walking around plant, making it possible to detect even small air leaks throughout plant.
Furthermore, after the inspection, we will provide a report containing detailed information such as photos of each air leak location, leak amount, and annual loss amount.
